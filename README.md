> **ResearchMind-AI v2** is an AI-powered Research Paper Intelligence System that combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), semantic search, Large Language Models (LLMs), and PDF processing to help researchers efficiently explore, understand, summarize, compare, and analyze scientific literature.

Unlike traditional keyword-based search systems, ResearchMind-AI understands the semantic meaning of research papers using dense vector embeddings generated by Sentence Transformers and performs high-speed similarity search using FAISS. The retrieved context is then combined with Groq-powered Large Language Models to generate accurate and context-aware responses.

# рџ“– Overview

Research papers are growing at an unprecedented rate, making it increasingly difficult for students, researchers, and professionals to stay up to date.

ResearchMind-AI addresses this challenge by providing an intelligent research assistant capable of:

- Performing semantic search across thousands of research papers.
- Answering research questions using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
- Summarizing lengthy research papers.
- Comparing multiple research papers.
- Extracting important keywords automatically.
- Generating professional literature reviews.
- Creating academic citations in multiple formats.
- Maintaining conversation history.
- Exporting generated outputs.

The system is built with a modular architecture, making it easy to extend with new AI capabilities and research tools.
